AHMEDABAD

Ahmedabad, the heart of Gujarat in India, is a citythat blends history, culture, ane modernity in a unique way. As the first UNESCO World Heritage City of India, it is home to archiectural marvels like the intricately design Adalaj Stepwell and the stunning Jama Masjid. The Sabarmati Ashram, once the residence of Mahatma Gandhi, offers a glimpse into the India's independence movement and Gandhi's philosophy of non-violence. The city's vibrant streets are filled with the aromas of mouthwatering street food, from khaman dhokla to fafda-jalebi, making it a paradise for food lovers. Additionally, Ahmedabad hosts the world-famous Kite Festival (Uttarayan), where the sky fills with thousands of colourful kites, creating an unforgettable spectacle.
